Special Circumstances

Intellectual Consultancy

I have a brain and, for as long as I can remember, people have been trying to convince me that it's actually a pretty good one. I am skeptical, as I am wont to do, but since so many people think it's a good brain, I have been loaning it out lately. I call this "Intellectual Consulting" and it's actually a pretty sweet gig. The easiest way to put it is thusly: if you need someone to think about something and then tell you what they thought, I can do that. I'm absurdly good at it, and I can think about all sorts of things; sometimes I can think about two or three things at once!

I'll have more detailed information about the types of things I can think about for you here at a later date. I'll also include what sort of things I can produce for you as a result of that brainpower. It's stuff like proposals, reports, and advice; but it's also stuff like an infrastructure upgrade project, a custom-built piece of code to do that one thing that you desperately need done but can't find a retail product to do for you, or a fully-customized home or business automation system that doesn't rely on third-party servers to keep all your stuff doing what you want it to do.
